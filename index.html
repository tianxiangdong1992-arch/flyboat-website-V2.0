About Flyboat

National High-Tech Enterprise with Strong R&D Capability

Guiyang High-Tech YiGe Electronic Co., Ltd has been a designated "special manufacturer of MOVs for SPD and onboard SPD" for over two decades. We serve as a drafting committee member for China's national varistor standard.

Our facility spans 9,000+ m² with modern production lines capable of producing approximately 20 million standard MOV units and 5 million onboard SPDs annually.
