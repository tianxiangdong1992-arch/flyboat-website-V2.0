About Flyboat

National high-tech enterprise with over 20 years of expertise in surge protection manufacturing.

Who We Are

GuiYang Hi-Tec YiGe Electronic Co., Ltd. (formerly Guizhou Flyboat Electronic Co., Ltd., est. 2000) is a globally recognized manufacturer of Surge Protective Devices (SPDs) and Metal Oxide Varistors (MOVs). Over the past two decades, we have built robust R&D capabilities, serving clients across 80 countries and regions. In 2025, we achieved a milestone by fully digitalizing and upgrading our production to an intelligent manufacturing system.

Industry Leadership & Standards

We are a designated National High-Tech Enterprise and a Provincial "Specialized and Innovative" SME. As a core drafter of China's national varistor standards, we are at the forefront of industry development. Notably, our Legal Representative, Mr. Zihao Fei, serves as a Chinese Expert on the IEC SC37B Sub-Committee and deeply participated in revising the international standard IEC 61643-331 (2017).

Manufacturing Excellence

Operating from a 6,600+ m² site with over 9,000 m² of modern industrial space, we are engineered for high-volume supply. Our advanced production lines deliver an impressive annual capacity of 20 million standard MOVs and 5 million onboard SPDs.
